About Us

We are a Juakali industry based in Nairobi, Kenya, specializing in handmade metal products.

Our team of skilled artisans uses traditional techniques to create high-quality and unique metal products that are both functional and beautiful.

At Juakali Industry, we are committed to sustainability and ethical practices, and we source our materials locally to support the community.

Thank you for considering our products, and we look forward to serving you!
